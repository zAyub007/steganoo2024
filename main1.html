The Steganographie Story

Once upon a time, Lucas discovered his ancestors' secret and invited his best friend to reveal his findings!

Lucas: "It all began when my father gave me this magic book and told me not to open it until he passed away."

You: "And what's this book about?"

Lucas: "It's a practice of hiding information within another message or object, so its presence isn’t obvious. Our family created it to connect secretly."

You: "You mean like in the movies?! I can't wait to see what the book reveals!"

Lucas: "Haha, exactly like the movies! And Marvel stories. You’ll not only see it; I’ll teach you how to wield this power."

You: "Why me, though?"

Lucas: "Because you're part of the Cybersicca community and my best friend. Together, we can change the world."

In memory of our heroes.
